I helped a national franchise triple revenue by redesigning their sales process. We replaced a two-week estimate turnaround with an on-the-spot iPad signing system — removing friction at the exact moment customers were ready to decide.

Outcome: faster closes, higher conversion, immediate cash flow lift.

I worked with a 5-year-old business stuck at $500k/month. By restructuring customer service upsells, tightening outreach cadence, and fixing follow-up timing, we scaled them to $1.2M/month in 60 days.

Outcome: same demand, same team — dramatically higher revenue per customer.

This is the pattern behind every IMPACTA engagement: diagnose, remove friction, execute fast.
